About Me ˄

Had my early schooling in my village at Panchayat Union Elementary School (2002-07) and Government Higher Secondary School, Yethapur, Salem, Tamil Nadu (2007-14). Cleared Tamil Nadu Rural Students Talent Search Examination (TRUST) in 2011, Used to participate in Essay, Oratorical competitions in Tamil.
